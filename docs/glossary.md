# Glossary

This glossary provides definitions for key terms and concepts used throughout the book.

## A

### AI-Driven Development (AIDD)
A software development methodology where artificial intelligence assists or automates various stages of the development lifecycle, from requirements gathering to testing and deployment.

## S

### Spec-Driven Development (SDD)
A methodology where detailed specifications (specs) serve as the primary source of truth, driving the development process and enabling automated generation of code, tests, and documentation.
